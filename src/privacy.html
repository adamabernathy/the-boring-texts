Privacy Policy

Overview

The Boring Texts is a fully client-side tool. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains what data we do — and do not — collect.

No Data Collection

This tool runs entirely in your browser. The company name, year, contact email, and any other information you enter into the form are processed locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, stored in any database, or shared with any third party.

No Cookies

This site does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, or any other browser-based persistence mechanism to track or store your information.

No Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or any other analytics or tracking technology on this site.

Third-Party Services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). Google may collect standard connection information (such as your IP address) when serving these fonts. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

No other third-party services, scripts, or resources are loaded.

GitHub Pages Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as your IP address as part of standard web server operations. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page. No prior notice is required.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may open an issue on the GitHub repository.
